Biography

I am a PhD student in the group of Tom Sheppard at University College London. My daily laboratory routine usually involves designing and setting up experiments, analysing the results and planning follow-up experiments if all goes well.

Before joining UCL, I completed my Integrated Masters in Natural Sciences from the University of Leeds where I majored in Chemistry and also studied Mathematics. Outside of the lab, I enjoy watching Formula 1 racing and I like to spend some of my free time playing badminton (though poorly I admit!).
