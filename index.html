Frequently Asked Questions

I often find it hard to save. How does this app help?

The app makes it easy for you to see the impact your actions have on your saving goals. Reach your target and buy guilt-free what you have saved up for. Simple yet highly motivating!

Is my saving data secure?

Yes! Your date is stored on your device only. It never is passed over the internet which helps make it more secure.

Can I add in past savings that repeat every month?

Sure! Add in past and future repeating savings too with a press of a button. Just add in a start date, how often it repeats and how long you want it to repeat for. Then sit back and relax!

What are some examples of savings I can quickly make?

Why not try haggling for a cheaper mobile contract or TV subscription when it comes to renew? Also, consider spending less on brand-new clothes and try renting out clothes instead?

Is this app really free?

Yes, totally free to use! The creator of the app (Usman) had built it for himself and found it so useful he improved it further for everyone to use, free of charge!
